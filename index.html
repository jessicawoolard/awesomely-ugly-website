Sloths Are Awesome!




What is a sloth you ask?

Sloths are animals that live in tropical rainforests of South America and Central America. They are known as the slowest mammals alive. The sloth is so named because of its very low metabolism and deliberate movements, sloth being related to the word slow.

What does a sloth eat?

Leaves are what they crave. It will take about 25 days to digest one leaf. Sloths use the bathroom only once a week!

What do sloths do?

Captured sloths tend to sleep most of their day (about 15-20 hours) and do not have much time for socializing. They spend their time in trees and sleep hanging upside down.


Where did they come from?

Sloths belong to the superorder Xenarthra, a group of placental mammals believed to have evolved in the continent of South America around 60 million years ago. One study found that xenarthrans broke off from other placental mammals around 100 million years ago.
